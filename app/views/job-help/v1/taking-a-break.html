Before making any decisions, find out what additional support could be available. Consider what support you could get from your employer to help you stay in work or explore our tips for finding a flexible new job that suits you.

Try to plan for any potential impacts on things like finances or pensions before you decide to leave work. Leaving work also doesn’t have to be a permanent decision. Consider how you could stay in touch with your employer to help you return to work in the future.

Find out more about what to consider before leaving work on Carers UK. For confidential advice and information, call Carers Direct on 0300 123 1053 (Monday to Friday, 9am to 8pm; Saturday and Sunday 11am to 4pm) or find other helplines on NHS.UK.
