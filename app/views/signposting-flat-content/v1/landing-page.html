If you regularly spend time caring for another adult because they're ill, they're an older person or they're disabled – including family members, there are resources to support you.

You may also find this page useful for planning ahead, if you think you might be regularly caring for another adult in the future.

If you are working and caring for someone

If you are working and caring for someone, there may be options available to you, including professional care, wider support services, sharing care with friends and family, flexible working and other workplace rights and entitlements.
